You can request prescriptions online by logging into your online account, by post, in person or on the NHS App. Admin staff cannot take telephone requests even when the item required is a repeat prescription.

We require 48 hours to process repeat prescriptions. Where possible we email them to the Pharmacy on the same day.

The current prescription charge if you are not exempt is £9.35. If you have more than 3 prescriptions in 3 months you may benefit from a Pre-Payment Certificate. Your Pharmacist will have a form for this.

The dispensary on-site is for patients who live 3 miles away or more from the surgery.

A Dr will need to review your repeat medications once a year. Please make an appointment once a year for this or we will not be able to issue your repeat prescription, this can be a telephone appointment for most patients.
