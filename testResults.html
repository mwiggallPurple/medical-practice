If your test results require discussion or action we will telephone you or send you a text asking you to telephone us on 01234 567 890

Our blood test clinic is open every day 08:00 until 12:30. No appointment is necessary. The blood has to go pathology promptly so no afternoon appointments will be available.

You can also visit the phlebotomy clinic at any local hospital for blood tests. As their laboatory is on-site afternoon appointments are available. No appointment is necessary.
