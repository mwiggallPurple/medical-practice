Getting the Best From Your Appointment


Before the Consultation It is usually best to concentrate on one problem during consultation, If you want to discuss several problems or family members, please ask the receptionist for a separate appointment.

Think, When did the problem start?
Does anything make the problem better or worse?
How would you describe it?
What worries you more, the discomfort or what might be causing it?
How are you hoping the doctor might be able to help you?
If you think you (or your child) may have to be examined, please wear clothes that are easily removed.

If you are alone and would like a chaperone please tell reception when you check in for your appointment and one will be provided.
