  • We now have clinics available for patients due an autumn covid booster and a flu vaccination booster.
    If you are 65 or over please ring 01234 567 890 to book an appointment. Other eligible patients are;

  • Immunosuppressed (on chemotherapy, having radiotherapy, transplant recipients, HIV Positive, Multiple myeloma
    or genetic disorders affecting the immune system. History of Leukaemia, Lymphoma. Long term immunosuppressive treatment
    for conditions including Lupus, Rheumatoid arthritis, IBS, Scleroderma and Psoriasis.
  • Household contacts of immunosuppressed people.
  • Carers, including people who care for relatives or friends.
  • Pregnancy.
  • Health & Social Care workers.
  • Asplenia.
  • Severe mental illness.
  • Morbid Obesity BMI over 40.
  • Chronic respiratory disease. COPD, Cystic Fibrosis, Emphysema, Asthma needing steroid inhalers.
  • Chronic liver disease.
  • Chronic kidney disease, stage 3,4 & 5.
  • Chronic heart disease.
  • Diabetes.
  • Chronic neurological conditions, Stroke, TIA, Cerebral Palsy, Down's Syndrome, Multilpe Sclerosis, Epilepsy, Dementia, Parkinson's Disease,
    Motor Neurone Disease.

  • Childrens Nasal Influenza Vaccinations.
